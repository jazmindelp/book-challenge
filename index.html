About the book

When Pessoa died in 1935, he left behind a trunk filled with unfinished and unpublished writings, among which were the remarkable pages that make up his posthumous masterpiece The Book of Disquiet
This perpetually unclassifiable and unfinished book of self-reflective fragments was first published in Portuguese in 1982, and grapples with all the eternal questions.
